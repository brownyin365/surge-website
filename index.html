    About SurgeForks

    SurgeForks is the ultimate multi-utility DeFi project built on top of SurgeProtocol. Our goal is to bring more decentralization to the DeFi space by launching our own token, $SRG-F, paired with $SRG. We aim to connect and contribute to the growth of the entire Surge ecosystem while also exposing the DeFi community to the Surge ecosystem.

    Our use cases include

    • SurgeForks List and Monitor

      SurgeForks List and Monitor - A professional-looking monitor page where all listed Surge projects can be easily viewed by the Surge community and the entire DeFi space. Additionally, there will be banner ads available for rent to Surge projects, with payment in $SRG-F tokens.

    • SurgeForks Marketing Pool

      SurgeForks Marketing Pool - Using our treasury, we will directly buy a specific amount into all incubated Surge projects that will be listed on our monitor. A pool will be created where all tokens will be domiciled and used for marketing to the entire DeFi community through weekly shill and giveaway campaigns, as well as periodic buy and burn campaigns.

    • SurgeFork Miner Pool

      SurgeFork Miner Pool - As part of our roadmap, we will launch a unique mining pool that allows holders of Surge projects to directly stake into it and earn $SRG-F tokens as rewards. Details on daily income, deposit and withdrawal fees will be released.

    • SurgeFork Lottery Game

      SurgeFork Lottery Game - We will release a unique multi-token lottery game, where any approved DeFi tokens, as well as all SRG-listed tokens, can be used for the lottery game. Lottery tickets will cost $5 worth of any SRG token per ticket.
